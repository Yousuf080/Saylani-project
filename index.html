We help To Pakistan Poor And Deserving Peoples

Saylani Welfare International Trust, based in Pakistan, is a non-profit organization focused on humanitarian efforts.

Food distribution offering meals through soup kitchens and food banks to combat hunger.

Healthcare services operating medical camps, clinics, and hospitals for those in need.

Education support running schools and vocational training programs for underprivileged children.
